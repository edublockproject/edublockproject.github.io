About EBL

EBL is a future-oriented blockchain education platform. It aims to provide quality vocational training for practitioners who want to enter the blockchain industry.

Direct-To-User Decentralize Platform

The decentralized value Internet is the future of mankind, which will certainly require a large influx of highly qualified talents.

The key to the success of any technology enterprise is to hire the best and well-educated talents, therefore, the success of blockchain enterprises requires a large pool of talents

ICO Problem & Solution

In our exploratory efforts, we gradually distilled the profile of users and sought solutions that would provide maximum benefits to all parties involved.

Problem

Origin Educational Platform
  • Centralized system with systemic risk.
  • The teaching content is outdated and cannot be adapted to the current market environment.
  • Trainees are not interested in the learning content and are not very effective after learning.

Solution

EBL Network Solution
  • Decentralized system with security.
  • Decentralized network, all participating members provide content output to guarantee the timeliness and usefulness of teaching content.
  • The Token incentive ensures that the trainees have the incentive of interest throughout the process, thus ensuring the learning interest and learning effect.
